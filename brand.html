02

Three Concepts

The system carries one primary mark and two supporting glyphs — for stamps, monograms, and small contexts where the star fort would lose detail.

A · primary

Star Fort

Four-bastion trace italienne. The plan view of a permanent fortification, reduced to its load-bearing geometry.

B · ordnance

Crossed Ordnance

Two cannon barrels saltire through a cannonball. A heraldic stamp for letterheads, seals, and footers.

C · monogram

F / O Monogram

Slash lockup of the initials. A nod to the BOF/ARCHIVE wordmark already in the terminal.

03
